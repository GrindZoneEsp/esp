Refund Policy

  • 1. For refunds, you must provide genuine proof of transaction or mishap.
  • 2. Your registration can only be cancelled up to 60 minutes before the tournament start time. After that, cancellations are not allowed.
  • 3. For refunds, message us on our socials:
    • YouTube
    • Instagram
    • Discord
  • 4. Refunds can take up to 12+ hours to process.
  • 5. Don't worry, we are always ready to support you!

Tournament Rules

  • 1. Room ID and password will be shared 5 minutes before the start time.
  • 2. Please join on time. We are not responsible if you’re late.
  • 3. Maintain good behaviour with the host.
  • 4. Do not invite random players to the room.
  • 5. Host has full authority to kick anyone who abuses or invites randoms.
  • 6. Host has the right to select maps based on chat/community votes.
  • 7. Do not invite or play with hackers.
  • 8. Keep your behaviour polite and respectful.
  • 9. If you catch anyone cheating, report it immediately.
  • 10. We follow international point rules (except Champions Rush).
  • 11. Ensure your teammates have all maps downloaded.
